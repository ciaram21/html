About Us

We first got started in January 2015 with the idea of wanting to be able to get all our favourite sweets and treat in the same place. We make everything fresh in the shop daily. Our head baker studied at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu in London. All our bakers have a huge passion for making sweets and desserts.

We may be small but we offer big flavours. We have won the Small Business Award for East Midlands for the past 3 years and the Best Sweets shop in 2018.

There are many sweets shops around but we are the only place you can get your fresh baked goodies and sweets in the same place in Nottingham. We cater to customer's different dietary restrictions so if you're vegan or gluten free, we got some stuff for you too.
