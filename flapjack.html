Flapjack




Ingredients

  • 250g porridge oats
  • 125g butter
  • 125g light brown sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp golden syrup



Instructions

  • Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6. Put the oats, butter, sugar and golden syrup in a food processor and pulse until mixed â€“ be careful not to overmix or the oats may lose their texture.
  • Lightly butter a 20 x 20cm baking tin and add the mixture. Press into the corners with the back of a spoon so the mixture is flat and score into 12 squares. Bake for around 15 mins until golden brown.
